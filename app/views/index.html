Better writing, every day.

Day Write is a daily writing tool which helps you become a better writer simply by making you write every day. It's stupid simple, and not only does it make you a better writer, but it can help others, too.

Request an invite

Make it a habit

Day Write encourages you to write every day, with reminders if you need them. It automagically detects your writing habits and can determine when you're most likely to want to sit and write, giving you a gentle prod.

Quantity, not quality

Your goal is to write about three pages a day, regardless of content. The idea is to exercise the muscle which puts your thoughts into text. Write whatever you want, but just make sure you hit the mark, or it doesn't count.

Build up a streak

Using the Seinfeld Method, Day Write shows you the unbroken chain of days in which you have successfully written the alloted amount. For some reason, this really appeals to your crazy lizard-brain. Don't miss one!

Complete privacy

There's no way for anyone else to see what you write, ever, and we're proud of that. So go crazy. Write out your hopes, your dreams, your sorrows, your love letters, and your Dana Carvy fanmail drafts. Nobody's gonna see it but you.

Give yourself more incentive

Not quite making your goal of writing every single day? No problem! Day Write will happily punish you for it. Flip the switch and we'll charge you $0.99 for every day that you don't write. Sucks, don't it?! Better get to writing.

It's for the kids, man

Every month we take the profits and donate them to Mighty Writers, a non-profit which teaches kids (ages 7 to 17) to think and write with clarity so they can achieve success at school, at work and in life.
